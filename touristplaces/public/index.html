Famous Tourist Places of India

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar in Delhi is the tallest brick minaret in the world, built in 1193.

India Gate

A war memorial in New Delhi, India Gate commemorates Indian soldiers of WWI.

Gateway of India

Located in Mumbai, this iconic arch was built during the 20th century for King George V's visit.
