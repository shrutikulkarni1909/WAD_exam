Basic Tennis Rules

  • Matches are played as best-of-3 or best-of-5 sets.
  • A player must win 6 games by a margin of 2 to win a set.
  • The game uses a unique scoring system: 15, 30, 40, and game.
  • Players switch sides after every odd game.
